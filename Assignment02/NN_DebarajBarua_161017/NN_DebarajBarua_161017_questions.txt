Q: What are the different learning rules?
A:
(i)    Error-correction Learning,
(ii)   Memory-based learning,
(iii)  Hebbian learning,
(iv)   Competitive learning,

Q: What are the different mechanisms for Hebbian learning?
A: 
(i)    Time dependent mechanism
(ii)   Local mechanism
(iii)  Interactive mechanism
(iv)   Conjunctional or correlational mechanism

(i)    Pattern association
(ii)   Pattern recognition
(iii)  Function approximation
(iv)   Control
